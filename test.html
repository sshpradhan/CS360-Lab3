ICCU Arena

Idaho Central Credit Union Arena is an athletic arena in the northwest United States, located on the campus of the University of Idaho in Moscow, Idaho. It is the home of the Idaho Vandals of the Big Sky Conference and serves as the primary venue for men's and women's basketball.
